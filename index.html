Integrated Water Supply Solutions

AL WEJHA GROUP designs and implements complete water supply systems across Oman, from high-pressure transmission mains to localized distribution networks. Our turnkey solutions ensure reliable water delivery with minimal losses.

  • Overflow and level monitoring
